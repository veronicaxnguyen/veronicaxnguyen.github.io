About Girls in Tech

Girls in Tech is a nonprofit organization whose goal is to close the gender gap in tech. Founded in 2007, Girls in Tech has funded over 4,000 women entrepreneurs, had over 49,000 participants in their hackathons, and had over 65,000 participants in their coding bootcamps.
