About Girls in Tech

Girls in Tech is a nonprofit organization whose goal is to bridge the gender gap within the tech world. With over 60,000 members across the country, these chapters pledge to ensure that every voice is heard. Founded in 2007, Girls in Tech has funded over 4,000 women entrepreneurs; hosted over 49,000 individuals in hackathons; and held boot camps with over 65,000 participants. Upholding the values of diversity and inclusivity, this organization stays passionate in bringing out the confidence, power, and potential within each woman.
